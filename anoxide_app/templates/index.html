Biography (long)

Antzaat was founded in 2015 by Ronarg, Isaroth and Eenzaat. In 2017, Nihil joined the band.

The Antzaat sound can best be described as old-school Scandinavian black metal, similar to bands like Sargeist and Behexen. On the 25th of September 2017, Antzaat released their EP "The Black Hand of the Father" with Immortal Frost Productions.

One year later Antzaat embarked on a European tour with Azaghal, Myrkvid and Sekhmet.

On the 30th of October 2020, Antzaat unleashed their first full length "For You Men Who Gaze into the Sun".

Biography (short)

Antzaat was founded in 2015. Their sound can best be described as old-school Scandinavian black metal. In 2017, Antzaat released their first EP and on the 30th of October 2020 they released their first full-length.
